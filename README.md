 <hr>
Objective<br>
To analyze and gain business insights from Amazon Prime Video’s content library, including Movies and TV Shows — with focus on:<br>
•	Content quantity<br>
•	Genres<br>
•	Ratings<br>
•	Directors<br>
•	Countries<br>
•	Release year trends<br>
 <hr>
Dataset Overview<br>
•	Source: Amazon Prime Video dataset<br>
•	Time Period: 1920–2021<br>
•	Key Columns:<br>
o	Title<br>
o	Type<br>
o	Genre<br>
o	Rating<br>
o	Director<br>
o	Country<br>
o	Release Year<br>
 <hr>
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)<br>
•	Total Titles: Overall count of Movies + TV Shows<br>
•	Total Ratings: Average rating across all titles<br>
•	Total Genres: Number of unique genres<br>
•	Total Directors: Number of unique directors<br>
•	Start–End Year: 1920–2021<br>
These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of Amazon Prime’s content ecosystem — its size, diversity, and creator base.<br>
 <hr>
Ratings by Total Shows<br>
A bar chart illustrates how content is distributed across rating categories.<br>
Insight:<br>
Amazon Prime mainly targets mature audiences, aligning with global trends toward adult-oriented dramas and thrillers.<br>
 <hr>
Genres by Total Shows<br>
A genre distribution chart shows that Drama, Comedy, and Documentary dominate.<br>
Insight:<br>
Prime focuses on emotionally engaging and real-life storytelling genres.<br>
 <hr>
Shows by Country<br>
A world map visual highlights the countries contributing the most content.<br>
 <hr>
Movies vs TV Shows<br>
A pie chart compares content types:<br>
•	Movies: ~65%<br>
•	TV Shows: ~35%<br>

Insight:<br>
The content library is primarily movie-driven, but TV shows are growing steadily.<br>
 <hr>
Business Insights<br>
1.	Content growth increased significantly after 2000 with Amazon Prime’s expansion.<br>
2.	Majority of content targets mature viewers.<br>
3.	Drama and Comedy are the most popular genres.<br>
4.	USA and India are the top content producers.<br>
5.	Movies dominate, but TV shows are seeing consistent growth.<br>
 <hr>
Conclusion & Recommendations<br>
•	Amazon Prime’s content library has grown rapidly since 2015.<br>
•	Focus on high-performing genres like Drama and Comedy for better engagement.<br>
•	Investing more in regional content can strengthen global reach.<br>
•	Maintaining balance between movies and TV shows helps attract a wider audience.<br>
